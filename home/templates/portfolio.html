DjentJam

DjentJam was my final project for Coding Dojo. The idea was to create a social site for people who are into the niche genre of metal known as "Djent". Users can register and login so they can make posts and store information in the websites database. Users can make posts, comment on posts, like posts, edit posts, edit comments, delete posts, and delete comments. However, I designed it to where they cannot delete anoyone elses posts, only their own. It's still a work in progress. I plan on adding more customizing options in the future such as adding more information to your profile and even uploading media. Check it out by clicking the link below!
